My Recipes

These are some of my recipes. I wanted to keep variation in my food so that customers can explore and enjoy different types of cuisines.

Green Salad

Green Salad is one of our popular healthy veggie item. It has tomato, avacado, pumpkin, chick peas, cabbage, lettuce etc. Then we top it off with our special sauce.

Pumpkin Soup

This is a delicious pumpkin soup with just enough spice for a mild kick. It's made with coconut oil instead of butter and uses light coconut milk in place of milk or cream. It's a great starter still quite filling.

Pesto Pasta

This is a tomato spinach pesto pasta. This is our on spot made italian pasta. It's mixed with fresh tomato and spinach.
